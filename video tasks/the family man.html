THE FAMILY MAN


SEASON 1
EPISODE 1
The Family Man S1 E1

The Family Man: Season 1 was showcased at the Television Critics Association's summer press tour held in Los Angeles in July 2019,[6][7] and was eventually premiered on Amazon Prime Video on 20 September 2019.[1] It received acclaim from critics and audiences, praising the performance of the cast members, and writing and execution. It eventually became the most viewed streaming series on Amazon Prime Video. The second season was scheduled to be aired on 12 February 2021 but was delayed, and it was finally released on 4 June 2021 and the season 3 is expected to be released in 2024.[8][9]
